About Aranmula Metal Mirror

The Legendary Aranmula Kannadi

The Aranmula Metal Mirror, known as Aranmula Kannadi, is a unique and ancient craft of Kerala. Unlike ordinary glass mirrors, this mirror is made from a special metal alloy polished for several days to achieve a crystal-clear reflection.

Each mirror is handcrafted by skilled artisans using a technique passed down through generations. This makes every piece a symbol of luck, purity, and tradition.
